"Musical Lines" Directions

  • Use your keyboard to enter characters (Note: Use backspace to delete keys)
  • Move the text around the screen with your curser. Use the up and down arrow to change the text size
  • To fix the text to your current location press the control key. Press the control key again to be able to move the text again.
  • Click the save png button to download a PNG of your name art.
  • Click the download video button to download of your name art.
